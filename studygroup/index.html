Study Group

The advanced algorithms study group at Academia Sinica is exactly what the name says, featuring a bunch of Sinica/NTU/NTHU students. We study together approximation/randomized/streaming/online algorithms together, following the general outline in Advanced Algorithms@ETHZ but incorporating more discussions on relevant research topics in, e.g., social network or networking. It is co-organized by two SH's: Shao-Heng Ko and Sheng-Hao Chiang.

Meeting time: we follow a 3-week rotating schedule (1 meeting per ~10 days): Week 3k+1: Monday 3:30 - 5:00 pm Week 3k+2: Friday 2:00 - 3:30 pm Week 3k: No meeting
