Advanced Algorithms Study Group

The advanced algorithms study group at Academia Sinica is exactly what the name says: just a bunch of people (from Sinica/NTU/NTHU/NTPU) studying algorithms together. Demographic is like 20% postdocs, 20% PhD students, 50% master students, and another 10% PIs. Alas, we are all learners in front of the algorithmic God. We leave anything within P behind as background, and study approximation/randomized/streaming/online algorithms together, following the general outline in Advanced Algorithms@ETHZ, but incorporating more discussions on the applications of the algorithmic techniques.

Coordinators: Shao-Heng Ko.

Meeting time: mostly on Tuesdays or Fridays in Spring 2020. Please see the detailed schedule below.
