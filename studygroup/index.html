Advanced Algorithms Study Group

The advanced algorithms study group at Academia Sinica is exactly what the name says: just a bunch of guys (from Sinica/NTU/NTHU) studying algorithms together. We study approximation/randomized/streaming/online algorithms together, following the general outline in Advanced Algorithms@ETHZ, but incorporating more discussions on the applications of the algorithmic techniques in, e.g., social network or networking.

Coordinators: Shao-Heng Ko and Sheng-Hao Chiang.

Meeting time: every other Thursday 8pm Online for now (from 12/5/2019).
